Investment Warden

Investment warden service consists of automated investor dashboard and a corporate governance expert appointed as a board member. It protects the investors' interest in a company and lowers the risks in private equity investments. Raised capital is held on an escrow account and released to the company based on agreed milestones.

Benefits
  • If the business plan fails, the raised capital can be partially reclaimed from the escrow
  • Automated Key Performance Index (KPI) reports for the investors
  • An appointed board member with full access rights who writes a summary to investors each month or quarter, so investors have an independent view of a company state
The service is ideal for:
  • Crowdfunded companies
  • Private companies with a high number of shareholders
  • Developing country companies who are looking invesments from abroad
